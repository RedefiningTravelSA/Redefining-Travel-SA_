Virtual Tours & Storytelling

Preview local experiences and learn about the history and culture before booking.

  • Village Life Intro: Walk through a rural village with audio storytelling about its people and traditions.
  • Traditional Dance Preview: Watch a dance rehearsal and meet the performers.
  • Festival Highlights: Virtual glimpse into food, music, and art from local events.
  • Language Snippets: Hear common phrases and greetings in Setswana.
  • Safari Sneak Peek: See highlights of wildlife tours hosted by youth guides.
  • Historical Landmark Tour: Virtual tour of an old site with narration from community elders.
  • Meet the Hosts: Video intros from youth hosts describing their offerings and cultural roots.
